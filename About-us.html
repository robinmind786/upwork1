Who We Are

Diaspora Doctors aims to achieve health equity globally by offering a cultural competent approach to virtual care, allowing patients to easily consult with healthcare providers based on their cultural background, from the furthest corner of the world at anytime for a better health outcome.

Our Mission

Empowering everyone from every corner of the world to be happy and healthy by providing access to an unbiased cultural competent approach to healthcare thereby achieving health equity globally.

Our Vision

Our vision is to eliminate health disparities by providing global access to effective and compassionate virtual care for patients of all background.

Doctors from all specializations are listed within the portal.

Patients can:

  • Search for doctors as per different specializations;
  • Review their credentials & experience;
  • Check the cost of online consultations;
  • Book appointments by processing online payments;
  • Share their medical records with the doctors.

At the designated time of the appointment the doctor will initiate the video call and the patient will be invited to join it. Medical reports can be shared while the call is in progress, and the Doctor can create and upload prescriptions, which will be filed within the database and available to the patient for download, at the end of the consultation.

Both doctors and patients will be able to access their dashboards and review their respective appointment histories.

Thus, Diasporadoctors is the one-stop shop for all patients to:

  • Gain access to the most prominent doctors in their locality
  • Avoid waiting in long lines at medical offices and outside of clinics
  • Adhere to the safety and norms of social distancing

Get diagnosed from the comfort of your home through video calls and receive prescriptions instantly!

Join us in working towards a healthy and safe future for everyone!
